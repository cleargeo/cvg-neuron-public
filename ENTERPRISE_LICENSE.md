## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What happens to my code after license expires?
**A:** You can continue using code downloaded during the license period for your internal projects, but you will lose access to updates, support, and the enterprise repository.

### Q: Can I share the enterprise code with contractors?
**A:** Contractors working directly for your organization under NDA can access the code, but you cannot share it outside your organization.

### Q: Can I deploy CVG Neuron in multiple environments?
**A:** Yes, you can deploy in development, staging, and production environments within your organization.

### Q: Is the license per-user or per-organization?
**A:** Enterprise licenses are per-organization with a specified user count. Additional users can be added.

### Q: Do I get source code or just binaries?
**A:** You receive full source code access, allowing you to modify and customize the system.

### Q: Can I request custom features?
**A:** Yes, custom development can be arranged separately. Contact us to discuss requirements and pricing.

### Q: What if I find a security vulnerability?
**A:** Report it immediately to azelenski@clearviewgeographic.com. We provide patches on a priority basis for enterprise clients.

### Q: Can I use CVG Neuron for client projects?
**A:** Yes, you can use it to deliver services to your clients, but you cannot redistribute the code itself.
